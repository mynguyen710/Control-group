Dataset Distribution

Legitimate Transactions 66%
Money Laundering 33%

For every 3 transactions in our dataset:

  • 2 are legitimate business transactions
  • 1 is identified as money laundering
This 2:1 ratio helps our system learn the difference between normal and suspicious patterns.

Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of financial transactions with the following columns:

Transaction Details

  • Timestamp: Date and time of the transaction
  • From Bank: Bank initiating the payment
  • Account: Sender's account ID
  • To Bank: Bank receiving the payment
  • Account.1: Receiver's account ID
  • Amount Paid: Transaction amount sent
  • Amount Received: Transaction amount received
  • Payment Format: Method of payment
  • Is Laundering: Label indicating if it's laundering

Currency Information

  • Payment Currency: Currency used to send payment
  • Receiving Currency: Currency received

Understanding Performance

When you create rules, you'll see these performance indicators:

  • Precision: How accurate your rule is when it flags suspicious transactions
  • Recall: How many actual suspicious transactions your rule catches
  • F1 Score: Overall effectiveness (balance of precision and recall)
  • Conflict Rate: How often rules disagree with each other
